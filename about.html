About Azuri Space Solutions

Smart solutions for every space, blending innovation, durability, and style for Nairobi homes and businesses.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Azuri Space Solutions started with a vision to transform everyday spaces into functional and stylish environments. What motivated us was the need to provide practical, innovative, and durable solutions for Nairobi homes and businesses.

Since then, we have grown steadily, building strong relationships with our clients and delivering high-quality carports, shade structures, and stainless steel & glass installations. Our dedication to excellence and innovation has allowed us to expand our services and reach a growing clientele.

Our Mission

To deliver smart, reliable, and aesthetically appealing solutions that enhance and protect every space, making it functional and stylish.
